Create the UI design of the e-commerce website, including:

Header:
Two-part header with a logo, search box, cart, and login buttons.
Navigation bar with links to products and categories.

Footer:
List of categories and links with a copyright message at the end.

Home Page:
Cover image with the company’s slogan.
Slider listing featured products.

Product Pages:
All products page in a grid format.
Women’s category page with sub-categories.
Men’s category page with sub-categories.
Kids category page.

Contact Page:
User-friendly contact us page requesting name, email, and message.

Login Page:
Login page requesting user’s email and password.

Cart Page:
Cart page listing products with quantity control buttons and delete option.

1. Header:

Ensure responsiveness for various devices.

2. Footer:
Simple grid layout with columns for Women, Men, Kids, and Links.
Responsive design for different screen sizes.

3. Home Page:
Cover image with aligned logo and slogan.
Featured products slider with responsive design for different devices.

4. Product Pages:
Consistent grid format for all products.
Sub-category pages for Women, Men, and Kids.

5. Contact Page:
Form to collect user’s name, email, and message.

6. Login Page:
Form to collect user’s email and password.

7. Cart Page:
Display products with quantity control buttons and delete option.
Design elements for future functionality.
